## 求集合的dom和ran

1.  
    $A \cup B = \{<1, 2>, <1, 3>, <2, 4>, <3, 3>, <4, 2>\}, A \cap B = \{<2, 4>\}$

    $$
    \begin{align*}
    dom(A \cup B)
    &= dom(\{<1, 2>, <1, 3>, <2, 4>, <3, 3>, <4, 2>\}) \\
    &= {1, 2, 3, 4} \\
    ran(A \cap B)
    &= ran(\{<2, 4>\}) \\
    &= {4}
    \end{align*}
    $$


## dom的证明

1.  
    $$
    \begin{align*}
    dom(R \cup S)
    &= \{x|(\exists y)(<x, y> \in (R \cup S))\} \\
    &= \{x|(\exists y)(<x, y> \in R \lor <x, y> \in S)\} \\
    &= \{x|(\exists y)(<x, y> \in R) \lor (\exists y)(<x, y> \in S)\} \\
    &= \{x|x \in dom(R) \lor x \in dom(S)\} \\
    &= dom(R) \cup dom(S)
    \end{align*}
    $$

2.  
    $$
    \begin{align*}
    dom(R \cap S)
    &= \{x|(\exists y)(<x, y> \in (R \cap S))\} \\
    &= \{x|(\exists y)(<x, y> \in R \land <x, y> \in S)\} \\
    dom(R) \cap dom(S)
    &= \{x|(\exists y)(<x, y> \in R) \land (\exists y)(<x, y> \in S)\} \\
    \end{align*} \\
    \begin{align*}
    \\(\forall x)((\exists y)(<x, y> \in R \land <x, y> \in S) \to ((\exists y)(<x, y> \in R) \land (\exists y)(<x, y> \in S)))
    &\Leftrightarrow (\forall x)(x \in dom(R \cap S) \to x \in (dom(R) \cap dom(S))) \\
    &\Leftrightarrow dom(R \cap S) \subseteq dom(R) \cap dom(S)
    \end{align*}
    $$

## 复合关系的分配律

1.  
    $$
    \begin{align*}
    R \circ (S \cup T)
    &= \{\langle x, z \rangle | (\exists y)(\langle x, y \rangle \in R \land \langle y, z \rangle \in (S \cup T))\} \\
    &= \{\langle x, z \rangle | (\exists y)(\langle x, y \rangle \in R \land (\langle y, z \rangle \in S \lor \langle y, z \rangle \in T))\} \\
    &= \{\langle x, z \rangle | (\exists y)((\langle x, y \rangle \in R \land \langle y, z \rangle \in S) \lor (\langle x, y \rangle \in R \land \langle y, z \rangle \in T))\} \\
    &= \{\langle x, z \rangle | (\exists y)(\langle x, y \rangle \in R \land \langle y, z \rangle \in S) \lor (\exists y)(\langle x, y \rangle \in R \land \langle y, z \rangle)\} \\
    &= (R \circ S) \cup (R \circ T)
    \end{align*}
    $$
